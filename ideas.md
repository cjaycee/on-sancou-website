# ON Running Showcase — Design Ideas

## Approach 1: Swiss Precision Minimalism

<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style (Helvetica era) meets contemporary athletic performance

**Core Principles:**
- Absolute precision in spacing and grid — every element placed with mathematical intent
- Typography as the primary visual element, not decoration
- Monochromatic palette punctuated by a single bold accent (ON's signature red)
- Negative space used aggressively to let products breathe

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#0D0D0D) backgrounds with pure white text and ON's signature red (#E8002D) as the only accent. The red appears only on CTAs and logo — creating maximum visual tension.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric Swiss grid — content blocks offset to the left third, product imagery bleeding to the right edge. No centered layouts.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal rule dividers (1px, 20% opacity)
- Oversized tracking on uppercase labels (letter-spacing: 0.3em)
- Product images with stark white isolation backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states use subtle red underlines. Scroll triggers precise opacity transitions.

**Animation:** Staggered entrance animations on scroll — elements slide in from left 20px with 0.4s ease-out. No bouncing or spring physics.

**Typography System:** Neue Haas Grotesk Display for headings (fallback: system-ui), DM Mono for labels and prices, body text in 16px/1.6 line-height.

## Approach 2: Kinetic Athletic Brutalism

<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Sports Brutalism — raw energy meets technical performance

**Core Principles:**
- Bold, oversized type that commands attention
- Diagonal cuts and dynamic angles suggesting motion
- High-contrast black/white with electric accent colors
- Texture and grain to add tactile quality

**Color Philosophy:** Deep charcoal (#111111) base with OFF-WHITE (#F5F5F0) and a vivid electric yellow-green (#C8FF00) accent. The neon accent evokes ON's CloudTec technology and cutting-edge performance.

**Layout Paradigm:** Diagonal section breaks (clip-path), full-bleed imagery with text overlaid at aggressive angles. Products presented in a staggered masonry grid.

**Signature Elements:**
- Diagonal section dividers at 3-degree angles
- Bold weight type at 120px+ for hero headlines
- Product cards with hard drop shadows (no blur, offset 4px)

**Interaction Philosophy:** Cards tilt on hover (perspective transform). Scroll reveals use clip-path animations.

**Animation:** Fast, snappy transitions (0.2s). Product images scale to 1.05 on hover. Section reveals use clip-path expanding from left.

**Typography System:** Barlow Condensed (900 weight) for display, Space Grotesk for body, DM Mono for technical specs/prices.
</text>

## Approach 3: Elevated Editorial Athleticism ← SELECTED

<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** High-fashion athletic editorial — Vogue Sport meets technical performance gear

**Core Principles:**
- Editorial photography treatment with generous whitespace
- Sophisticated typographic hierarchy mixing serif and sans-serif
- Clean, airy layout that lets products be the hero
- Subtle motion that feels premium, not flashy

**Color Philosophy:** Warm off-white (#FAFAF8) background with deep charcoal (#1A1A1A) text. ON's red (#E8002D) used sparingly for CTAs only. Secondary palette of warm stone (#C4B8A8) for subtle accents.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-bleed hero with scroll-expansion effect. Below the fold: asymmetric two-column layouts alternating product-left/text-right. Shop section uses a horizontal scroll carousel.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin serif display font for product names (editorial feel)
- Generous padding (80px+ vertical rhythm)
- Product images with subtle warm-toned backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy:** Everything feels intentional and unhurried. Hover states are gentle (0.3s ease). The scroll-expansion hero is the centerpiece interaction.

**Animation:** Framer Motion for scroll-triggered reveals. Elements fade + translate Y 30px on entrance. The hero expansion is the signature animation.

**Typography System:** Playfair Display (700) for hero headlines, DM Sans (400/500) for body and navigation, DM Mono for prices and technical labels.
</text>
